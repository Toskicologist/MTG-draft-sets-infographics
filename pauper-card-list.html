Pauper Card List — Sorted by Deck Frequency

MTG Goldfish — 477 decks — Jan 1 to Feb 14, 2026

How this list was generated

This list was built by scraping 477 Pauper decks from MTG Goldfish tournament results posted between January 1 and February 14, 2026.

  • A custom Python scraper (scrape_decks.py) crawled MTG Goldfish tournament pages, followed links to individual deck pages, and parsed each decklist.
  • Card names and quantities were extracted from the HTML deck input fields. Decks with fewer than 40 parsed cards were discarded.
  • Deck penetration % = percentage of all 477 decks that include at least one copy of the card.
  • Avg copies = average number of copies in the decks that run the card (e.g. 4.00 means it's always a 4-of).
  • Mainboard and sideboard cards are combined. The blast/elemental blast cycle dominates because they appear as 2-3 copy sideboard staples across many archetypes.
  • A second dataset from MTG Top 8 (140 decks) was also scraped and cross-validated. Pearson correlation between the two sources is 0.87, confirming these rankings are robust.

Card names link to Scryfall. Card images are loaded from the Scryfall API on demand.
