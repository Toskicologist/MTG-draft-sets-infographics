How this comparison works

This page compares card popularity across three Magic: The Gathering formats using scraped tournament data:

  • Pauper: 477 decks from MTG Goldfish (Jan 1 – Feb 14, 2026)
  • Modern: 397 decks (deduped from 787) combining MTG Goldfish and MTG Top 8
  • Standard: 495 decks (deduped from 805) combining MTG Goldfish and MTG Top 8

Deck penetration % = percentage of all decks that include at least one copy of the card (mainboard + sideboard combined).

The histogram shows distribution of cards by penetration range. Click histogram bars to filter the table to that range. Use the format tabs to switch between formats. The comparison chart overlays all three distributions.
