Notes and Methodology for Grades

  • The Grades display assumes a normal distribution centered at C based on the selected win rate metric (GIH, OH, or GD). Each letter gradation (e.g. C vs. C+) represents a band of 0.33 standard deviations from the center. Based on our analysis of user tier lists, this most closely matches common usage in the Limited community.
  • Applying filters that affect card metric values (e.g. Format, Users, Decks, Date Range) will cause grades to be redistributed.
  • Applying filters that do not affect card metric values (e.g. Color, Rarity) will hide data but will not change the calculated grades.
  • Data shown in the preview hover reflects the currently applied filters.
